### About

**Data + Systems · Pace University (Data Science)** — I don't stop at notebooks. I take problems from **raw data → models → APIs → deployed products** people can actually use.

Right now that's **three live systems**: a **Datadog NYC hackathon** continuity agent ([DeadZone](https://deadzone-production-df6d.up.railway.app/)), an **8-agent founder compatibility** platform ([FounderFit](https://founderfit-production-2a73.up.railway.app/)), and **arXiv-scale research intelligence** over ~36K papers ([Eidon](https://eidon-web-001.onrender.com)). Same thread everywhere — **multi-agent orchestration**, NLP, FastAPI, and ship-to-prod on **Railway & Render**.

I've also worked at **real scale**: **2M+** consumer-complaint NLP, **100K+** multimodal video frames (conference research), **19K+** behavioral profiling, and **big-data ML on AWS EMR**. I like building where **data, product, and applied AI** meet.

**Open to collaborations** — and hiring conversations. Featured work ↓ · [more repos](#more-repositories) in the fold.
